About Me

From backpacking through South America, to running marathons in Peru, to developing web applications or playing with monkeys in the Amazon Jungle, It doesn't matter how hard what I'm doing seems to be, I always give it 100%. I've always loved working in high stress environments that can test what I'm capable of. I'm a very spontaneous, out of the box person who likes to bring new ideas to the table. Combining the zen of running with the problem solving aspect of software development is one of my favorite things to do. Whether it's Javascript, React, Redux, PostgreSQL, Flask, Python, Express or Sequelize, there's no bug a couple of miles can't help me solve!

Fitstagram

A full stack application inspired by Instagram for all things fitness!

Fitstagram allows you to keep up with your favorite atheletes by allowing you to follow them, like and comment on their pictures. You can also post your favorite workout photos and show off your progress. Discover new artists using the discover page or search for specific users using the searchbar.

La Table

An OpenTable style full stack application

Whether you are looking for the top rated restaurant in the LA area or if you're just looking for a quick bite of some good food, LA table will help you make reservations to keep your belly full. As a restaurant owner you can post your restaurants and create reservation slots for customers to use. As a user, you can make reservations, leave reviews and rate your experiences for others to see.

BeatFind

A Flickr style full stack application

Beatfind is a Flickr clone for everything concert related. Explore other users experiences with their favorite artists, create albums and post photos of the coolest event's you've ever been to!
